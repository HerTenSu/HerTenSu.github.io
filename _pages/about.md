# Zeyu Zhao

I am a **Postdoctoral researcher** in **Cyberspace Security** at the **School of Computer Science**, **Shanghai Jiao Tong University**.

My research lies at the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence**, **AI Security**, and **Adversarial Machine Learning**, with a particular focus on:

- Video adversarial examples
- Reversible adversarial examples
- Privacy-preserving adversarial learning
- Trustworthy and secure AI systems

I am currently working on research problems related to **AI security evaluation**, **LLM robustness**, and the **adversarial security of diffusion models**.
